Illustrator

Illustrator project for My Dogs.

Project Description & Results.

THE CLIENT

Max and Organa, they are two sweet pups. Max is a lab/golden mix, while organa is a pitbull/boxer mix.

THE PROPOSAL

I was going to take some of their best photos and turn them into drawings to use as backgrounds and other various things.

THE RESULTS

I ended up with more of a line technique for Max, showing off his beautiful face. For organa, it ended up being more realistic style, without eliminating the cartoonish feel.
