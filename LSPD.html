DESIGN

Ad designs and recreation for Lone Star Pediatric Dental.

Project Description & Results.

THE CLIENT

Lone Star Pediatric Dental is an Austin local dental practice with a focus on children.

THE PROPOSAL

They wanted an upgraded ad for their moms blog that had more information with still having a simplistic feel. They also needed a new ad for their loction in Lakeway.

THE RESULTS

For the mom's blog, I made a drastic change with adding different color palets with real life photos. For the Lakeway location, I made one ad for them which captures the lake feel, and another that can be used all year long, more similar to the mom's blog ad.
